Who We Are

Oakland K-9 is a 501(c) (3) non-profit organization with a focus on utilizing the exceptional abilities of trained detection dogs to protect our communities. Our organization operates on donations and partnerships, ensuring that our services remain accessible to any and all organizations in need of proactive security.

Our Mission

Oakland K-9 is committed to enhancing public safety by providing specialized detection services for explosives, firearms, and narcotics. Our highly trained canine teams are dedicated to ensuring the security of schools, hospitals, special events, and other critical environments.
