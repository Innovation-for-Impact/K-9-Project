Our Services

Explosives Detection

Our canine teams are trained to detect a wide range of explosive materials, providing an essential layer of security at events, public spaces, and sensitive locations.

Firearms Detection

To help prevent violence in schools, hospitals, and public gatherings, our dogs are trained to detect firearms, offering peace of mind to those in vulnerable areas.

Narcotics Detection

We assist in maintaining drug-free environments by deploying our canine units to detect illegal substances in schools, healthcare facilities, and during public events.

Community Safety Programs

In addition to detection services, we engage with the community through educational programs that promote safety awareness and responsible practices in schools and other institutions.

Why Choose Oakland K-9?

Expertise

Our canine teams are trained to the highest standards, ensuring accurate and reliable detection services. Our highly trained canine teams are dedicated to ensuring the security of schools, hospitals, special events, and other critical environments.

Community Focus

As a non-profit, our mission is centered around public safety and serving the community. We provide detection services across various settings, including educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and large-scale public events.
